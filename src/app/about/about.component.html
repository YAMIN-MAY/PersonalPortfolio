Here's My Story

I graduated from the University of Computer Studies in 2018 with a Bachelor of Computer Science in Software Engineering. My journey in tech began with an internship developer role in 2017, which provided valuable early experience in the field.

Following the internship, I launched my career as a junior developer, then advanced to a Software Application Developer role in the FinTech industry. In this position, I worked on payment systems and live banking applications, gaining specialized expertise in financial technology.

Today, I am working as a System Analyst in Singapore’s semiconductor industry.

Life Outside the Code

Beyond coding, I am passionate about traveling and landscape photography, capturing videos, and creating fun blogs to share my experiences. Staying active is essential to me—I love running, bicycling, swimming, and hiking.

I am also committed to making a positive global impact, participating in initiatives like tree-planting events. As an animal lover, I support animal rescue efforts to contribute to their welfare.
